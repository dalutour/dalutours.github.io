EXCURSIONS BY DaluTOURS

We take a lot of pride in showing guests our beautiful island, full of history, exciting boat trips and exotic culture. As conclusion are our guides work within DaluTours to guaranty our standards concerning quality and expertise. Even in the rare case, that our guides are all busy, we are only using selected guides on freelance basis. All guides are officially certified by ZATOGA, our Zanzibar Tour Guide Association.

The World Cheapest!

Find below a selection of our DaluTours’ excursion portfolio:

STONE TOWN: This is wonderful in Zanzibar and giving you a feeling of the rich culture heritage, and also we get a comprehensive view history and walk down the streere of the stone town ,magnificent,areiniture,narrow alley, sand stone ancient built house where some of them are over 200 years old and have Arab and India carved doors the tour starts at the main city market which was opened in 1804 where tropic fruits, vegetables, spices fish from the ocean are sold. our next is Anglican church of the christ which was built by bishop Edward streere in 1872's on sits where the slaves to be caution. And other stone town historical sites as well. The tour includes transport if you stay out of the stone town, the stone town located West of the zanzibar island. If you stay north, east or south then we must include the transport. Lastly this is the half day tour so it is normaly takes two hours to two hours and a half from start to finish.

 

PRISON ISLAND:

Prison island or changuu island is the middle island of the three islands near around the stone town sea view. It takes aproximately 40 minutes to get there by a traditional dhow. The main atraction in this tour is the chromous giant tortoies. The tortoies where brought on Zanzibar from seychelies by sultan Said majid in the 1820's and another atraction is the prison which was never used on this built on 1873 was later a quarantine hospital apart from the prison ruins. On this little paradise one can also swim, snorkel or just relax on the beach and enjoy the peacefulness this small island has to offer. list but not last, this tour also include transport such as atraditional sailing dhow, taxi, and the snorkeling equipments if one prefers to snorkel.

SPICE FARM: The history of zanzibar would be incomplete without the cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, pepper and many other spices which brought the Sultans of Oman and the beginning of the infamous slave trade. They can be seen in the plantation just outside zanzibar town, and a good tour includes opportunities to dazzle the senses with fresh spices. A detailed description is given about a veriety of the spices, and their uses in cooking and cosmetics. visitors will be fascinated by the sheer number of spices produced and their incredible value for many ailment. This is also the cheapest place to purchase spice and spice oils.

JOZANI FOREST & BUTTERFLY CENTRE: During a guided tourthrough the Jozani National Park, guests will see the different species of monkeys, impressive trees and plants. Guest willuncover the magical world of the Zanzibari mangroves while walking along the wooden trail. This tour is combined with a visit to the Zanzibar Butterfly Centre to learn about the life cycle of a butterfly and see them flying in close proximity. We offer this tour as a half day tour or as a full day tour including local lunch in combination with Stone Town (Zanzibars Nature and its capital), Spice Farm (Fauna and Flora served on Spices), The Rock Restaurant (Fauna and Flora served on the Rock) or the Seaweed Centrein Paje (Zanzibars nature and community).

VILLAGE TOUR: This is the half day tour to the extreme north of the island there are number of very interesting things to see and do including the traditional fishing village itself with resident speaking a peculiar swahili dialect. then the turtle aquarium with various species. a traditional boat yard and local market are amaizing. at the end of the walk you can either relax on thesandy beaches or go swimming in the natural sea. this tour includes transpot weather the clients not stay in nothern of the island. in this tour foods,drinks and gifts. are a tourists resposibility him or her self.

SAFARI BLUE (the Original): Safari blue has bein running great day's out in Zanzibar since 1996. Safari blue starts from Fumba village,south west of Stone town. Fumba is the most convenient location for exploring the wild life, isolated sandbanks and rugged island scenery of the Menai Bay Conservation Area. "Safari blue is not just a day out on the traditional sailing dhow with good food. Come prepared to get your feet wet, enjoy new experiences, make new friends and most importantly, have the most memorable day of your life".

SEAFARI – MNEMBA ATOLL: After heading, with a dhow, from Kendwa to the stunning Mnemba Atoll – and with every possibility of seeing dolphins along the way, our guests will stop at Mnemba Kitchwani Reef and at Coral Gardens for snorkeling. This amazing reef with steep slopes offers some great under water topography and a chance to see amazing corals and a lot of different species of fish. A snorkel guide will also bein the water pointing out all of the little camouflage and the tricks most peoples eyes miss. At the end of the trip our guests will enjoy a mouthwatering lunch and relax on the beautiful secluded beach of Muyuni Beach.

TUMBATU ISLAND: Tumbatu is one of Zanzibars best kept secrets as the vast majority is forbidden from setting foot on the island. To enter the island you need special permission from the elders of the Tumbatu community. Although this is the island where, in the 11th century, the Shiraz built the first stone building in the entire Zanzibar Archipelago, you wont find cars or scooters as there isonly one road to walk on throughout the whole island. On thisfull day tour, leaving from Mkokotoni by boat, guestswill visit the school of Jongowe and the house of a fishermans family. Our guests will also be told about the daily life and the secrets of Tumbatu – as they are also famous for witch craft.

SEAWEED FARM: Visit the Seaweed Center to learn about Seaweed farming which is the second biggest industry on the island. Located in Paje, this social enterprise creates opportunities for female seaweed farmers to improve their standard of living and engage in economic development activities that benefit the whole community. Depending on the tide it is possible to see how the women harvest their seaweed. Our guests will visit theprocessing center and factory located in the village. Here theyll get the rare opportunity to see how the seaweed gets transformed into desirable beauty products like organic soaps and essential oils. We offer this experience as a half day tour or as full day tour combined with the Jozani Forest and Butterfly Center (Zanzibars nature and community).

SUNSET CRUISE: This 2 hour boat trip can either start from the Northern Coast of the Island, to watch the world famous sunset of Zanzibars North Coast or it can start from Stone Town where guestscan enjoy the best view of the capital from the water. Snacks and drinks are provided on the traditional wooden dhow and if the wind allows the crew, they will sail like the traditional fishing boats while you listen to traditional Zanzibar music.

THE ROCK RESTAURANT: The unique locationof this restaurant is an attraction in itself. If the tide is high, guests can use a boat to get to the restaurant – or even swim; and when the tide is low, they can simply walk.

NIGHT LIFE TOUR: This tour is done at nights, It starts at stone town to the place which called FOROZANI Gerden. Is located western of the stone tone and the main atrractions in this tour is to feel the freshness of sea breath and it's nice atmosphere which the Forozani gerden has to offers. Is not just having the relax and fresh air from the sea breath, come and make new friends new experiences with the nice tropical foods and drinks. And also in this tour there is an opportunitie for the one to go to Night clubs Parties, Festivals, Exhbitions and eid-el haj/fitr ceremonies.

DOLPHIN YOUR:

This is a half day tour the main atraction being able to swim close to the Dolphin. Being very social themselves, if you are lucky you even get to play with them. Situated on the southern point of the island, Kizimkazi fishing village is home to several schools of Bottle-Nosed Dolphins and Humback Dolphins, which are sighted following short boat trip from the village. the trip includes transport from either Stone Town or Hotel in the East or North Coast, guide,boat,snorkeling equipmemt and lunch..

The excursions' Prices are charged per-person.
